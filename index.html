Presented By: Utkarsh Nigam

About

Objective:
Learn customer patterns & trends through visualization of online sales data & derive actionable insights to create a better differentiated customer experience this year.
Approach:
Analyze the performance of non-holiday months i.e. January till October and compare them with holiday months i.e. November-December, and then focus on the holiday season trends.
Data Source: Kaggle
Date Range: January 2019 - December 2019

Following metrics/dimesnions have been used throughout this project:

  • Visits: Number of sessions across the website
  • Orders: Number of purchases made on the website
  • Revenue: Monetary amount (in $) of products purchased within all orders
  • Order Conversion Rate: Ratio of orders to visits
  • Propensity to Buy: Ratio of cart additions to visits
  • Holiday Sensitivity: Ratio of orders in holiday season (Nov-Dec) by orders in whole year
  • Peak Sale Days: Wednesday before Thanksgiving (27 Nov), Thanksgiving (28 Nov), Black Friday (29 Nov) & Cyber Monday (2 Dec)
  • Paid Traffic: Users coming from paid marketing channels such as Display Ads, Social Media
  • Unpaid Traffic: Users coming from unpaid marketing channels such as Organic Searches, Bookmarks.

To highlight the importance of Holiday Season, below is the comparison of average monthly visits, orders and revenue for holiday season and non-holiday season.

Peak Days - When does most holiday shopping occur?

Holiday period together contributes to 1/3rd of the total yearly revenue, and the peak sale days constitute 30% of revenue in the holiday months. As compared to average day during holiday period, the peak sale days generate 5x more revenue.

State Wise

Texas, California and Florida have emerged to be the top revenue generating states. Also, users across 75% of the states spend the most a day before Thanksgiving i.e., Wednesday.

Holiday Sensitive Categories

Electronic is the most holiday sensitive category with 41% of their annual sales happening during the holiday season, followed by Home & Furnishing and Beauty & Accessory. Also, Electronic is the top revenue generating category for the business suring the holiday season.
